Тектоника Bird **исключена** из первичного конвейера; её провал — непригодность метрики, не доказательство против серий. **Гипотеза о физически значимых глобальных сериях не подтверждается.**

## EN

**Research question:** Do physically meaningful multi-regional global series exist in the M≥6.5 catalog (1973–2026)? Four distinct tests (§1.1 table): permutation p = 0.0001 rejects **Poisson times only**; literature ETAS p ≤ 0.001 (N = 27 > mean ≈ 15.4); global-series hypothesis **not confirmed**; WLS control (App. B) p = 1.0 — falsification framing, not primary null.

**Primary ETAS null** is literature H&S 2003 (μ = 0.008, K = 0.08; **decoupled** from detector): mean ≈ 15.4, p_ETAS ≤ 0.001. Deviation is **not interpreted** as evidence for global series. Detector is liberal (142 windows → 47 merged). Bird tectonic heuristic **excluded** from primary pipeline; its failure shows metric unsuitability, not evidence against series. The global-series hypothesis is **not supported**.
