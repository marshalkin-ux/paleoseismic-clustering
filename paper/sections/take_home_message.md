## EN

**Research question:** Do physically meaningful multi-regional global series exist in the M≥6.5 catalog (1973–2026)?

**Key result:** under temporal ETAS (p_ETAS = 1.0, mean = 27.0), N_obs = 27 is consistent with the null; global spatiotemporal series are not confirmed — definitive assessment requires spatiotemporal ETAS (Ogata, 1998).

Three test layers (§1.1 table): **(a)** permutation p = 0.0001 rejects Poisson event times (expected with aftershocks); **not** proof of global chains. **(b)** primary temporal MLE ETAS: p_ETAS = 1.0, mean = 27.0 — N_obs **consistent** with catalog-calibrated null. **(c)** spatial linkage — **not tested**.
